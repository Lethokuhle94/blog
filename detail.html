NEY THE BEA IN STUDIO

Celebrity Gossip International News

Ney The Bae shares how she got started in the entertainment industry In a candid interview, Anele shed some light about her humble beginnings and describes how it was not all glitz and glam for her.
She mentions that she first fell in love with television presenting when she was back home in Newcastle while watching videos of Nontle Thema. This is where she decided to move to Johannesburg and making her dreams a reality.

“The reason why I got into TV presenting was to create a feeling for somebody watching. To make yo feel like you are away from your problems, you’re away from all of your troubles and you’re watching me take you on a journey. So that’s always something that I’ve always wanted to do.”

Ney The Bae’s fashion sense is just as refreshing as she is Every week, Zondo captivates her fans with her jaw-dropping looks and inimitable sense of style. The 24-year-old is known as a typical girly girl that has a weakness for bright and bold colours. Because of this, she’s cemented her brand as a household name. She clearly doesn’t shy away from showing off some skin, her social media feed is packed with blindsiding snapshots which got us hooked on her features.

Furthermore, the KZN native has put to test her business skills as founder of hair brand, Ney On Hair which specializes in weaves and wig making. And it makes sense for the star because she saw a gap in the market and decided to pursue this business venture.

Make sure to catch Anele Zondo Friday night on hit show Massive Music on Mzansi Magic at 21h30.
